Methodology and updates

We aim to keep calculators consistent with common financial definitions and update tools when formulas, edge cases, or presentation needs improvement.

Last updated dates appear on tool pages where relevant. If you spot an issue, review the assumptions on the page and cross-check with your lender/provider terms.

How results work

Most calculators use standard formulas and simple assumptions based on the inputs you provide. Results are estimates to help you understand tradeoffs. Actual offers, eligibility, rates, fees, and terms are set by providers and may differ from estimates.
