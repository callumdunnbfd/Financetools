How to use this calculator

  • Enter property price, term and deposit.
  • Add optional fees, fixed period and overpayments.
  • Press Calculate to generate payments and schedule.

Important

This is an estimate tool. Some lenders calculate interest daily and apply fees differently. Always confirm figures with your lender or adviser.

Your property may be repossessed if you do not keep up repayments on your mortgage.
